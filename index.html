Fed-BioMed

Open-Source Federated Platform for Healthcare Secure Collaboration

Open, transparent and trusted federated technologies for real-world healthcare applications

Medical data cannot move.
But medical research must.

Patient data cannot be freely shared

Healthcare institutions must protect privacy and trust

A single institution does not have enough data

Research requires collaboration across institutions

Healthcare institutions operate under strict responsibility.

  • Patient data must remain protected at all times.
  • Legal and ethical obligations limit data sharing.
  • IT systems and infrastructures vary across institutions.
  • Research collaboration requires clear governance and accountability.

At the same time, high-quality medical research increasingly depends on data from multiple institutions.

Balancing collaboration and responsibility remains a central challenge.

Move computation, not the data.

Enable secure collaboration across institutions, without compromising data privacy.

Connects distributed institutions in a secure federated network.
