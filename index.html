Healthcare institutions can collaborate on AI without moving patient data

Open, Transparent and Trusted Collaborative Learning for Real-World Healthcare Applications

Medical data cannot move.
But medical research must.

Challenges in Healthcare ➤

Patient data cannot be freely shared

Hospitals must protect privacy and trust

A single hospital does not have enough data

Research requires collaboration across institutions

Move the algorithm, not the data.

Fed-BioMed enables hospitals to collaborate by training models locally and sharing only aggregated results.

How Fed-BioMed works
1

Researchers send training plan

  1. Step 1: Researchers send training plan
  2. Step 2: Hospitals train data locally
  3. Step 3: The models are then securely aggregated and the final model is sent back to the institutions
